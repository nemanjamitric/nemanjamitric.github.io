<the road so far/>

<childhood/>

I was born in Subotica, Serbia, on the 16th of March 2001. Most of my childhood I've lived in a village called Čantavir near Subotica where I went to primary school. After finishing primary school I attended to the "Svetozar Marković" Gymnasium (grammar school) specializing in mathematics and natural sciences. This is where I really had my first contact with programming. We learned some programming basics in Pascal. I immediately fell in love with it and started taking extra classes. We also learned some HTML and CSS, but only the basics. I spent most of my energy during my high school years learning Biology and got to the national level of competition 3 times. After finishing high school with an average grade of 4.3, I wanted to study Informatics.

<high school/>

I'm started studying informatics at Subotica Tech College of Applied Sciences. I graduated with an average grade of 9.8 (which is pretty good in my opinion). I've competed in Bizkod in 2021, 2022 and 2023, which my team won twice. I also had a paid internship at ConcordSoft. At first I was a front-end developer working with Wordpress, but from the start of 2022 I am a Mobile App Developer working in React Native Expo. Currently I am working for the Camplin Group as a React Native Engineer.

<programming experience/>
